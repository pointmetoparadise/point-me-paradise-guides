Embarkation Day

Getting on the Ship

Check-in opens at 11:00 AM

The Galveston cruise terminal opens for MSC check-in at 11:00 AM. When you arrive at the port, porters will be outside to take your checked luggage — hand it off to them and it will be delivered directly to your cabin. Hold onto your carry-on backpack and anything you need for Day 1, as your checked bags may not arrive at your stateroom until mid-afternoon.

Pack an embarkation day backpack — your advisor's #1 tip

Your checked luggage may not arrive at your cabin until mid-afternoon. Pack a carry-on backpack with everything you'll want for Day 1 so you can head straight to the pool the moment you board.

What to pack:

  • All medications
  • Swimsuit & cover-up
  • Phone, charger & earbuds
  • Sunscreen
  • Cruise documents & ID
  • Change of clothes
  • Book, tablet or headphones

Bonus tip: This same backpack doubles as your overnight bag on the last night. Set your main luggage out for the porters, keep essentials in the backpack, and walk off the ship the next morning completely stress-free.

Specialty dining onboard

If you'd like to treat yourselves to a special night out, the Seascape has five specialty restaurants, all grouped together on Deck 8 so they're easy to browse before you decide. They carry an additional cost — you can order à la carte, choose a set Dining Experience menu, or buy a specialty dining package — and reservations are made once onboard through the app or at the hostess stand in the specialty area.

  • Butcher's Cut — a classic American-style steakhouse with premium cuts of beef, seafood, and an extensive wine list.
  • Kaito Sushi Bar — sushi, sashimi, and Japanese specialties served à la carte.
  • Kaito Teppanyaki — a tableside experience where the chefs cook right in front of you.
  • Ocean Cay — a seafood-focused restaurant with Mediterranean and coastal European dishes.
  • HOLA! Tacos & Cantina — Mexican-inspired street food with tacos, burritos, and house-made salsas.

MSC for Me App & Wristbands

Download the MSC for Me app before you leave home

Search MSC for Me in the App Store or Google Play and download it now. Once you board and connect to the ship's Wi-Fi, the app loads your personal sailing — every show, event, dining option, bar special, kids' activity, fitness class, and evening performance, all in one place, updated daily. Do not leave home without it.

MSC for Me wristbands (optional, highly recommended)

Waterproof bands that replace your cruise card for cabin access, onboard purchases, and muster check-in. Perfect for pool days. Typically, $14–$15 each or $21–$30 for a multi-pack — pick them up onboard.

Important: You'll still need your physical cruise card when leaving and re-boarding the ship in port. The wristband does not work at gangway security.

Booking shows & specialty dining onboard

Show reservations and specialty dining open once you're on the ship — all managed through the MSC for Me app. Check it early and often, especially for shows, as popular ones fill up fast.

Advisor Tips

From Your Travel Advisor

Pro Tip — Keep Your Room Cool

Your cabin's lights and A/C only run when a card is inserted into the slot near the door. Bring an old gift card, hotel key, or any similar-sized card from home and leave it in the slot while you're out — your room stays cool and ready when you return. Small thing, big difference on a hot Caribbean sailing!

Pro Tip — The Backpack Works Both Ways

On your last night onboard, set your main luggage outside your cabin door for the porters to carry off the ship. Keep just the essentials in your embarkation day backpack — you'll use it as your overnight bag and walk off the ship the next morning without any juggling required.

Pro Tip — Pack a Little Heat

MSC is an Italian line, so the cooking leans toward traditional European seasoning — flavorful, but milder than what many of us are used to back home. If you like your food with more of a kick, tuck a small shaker of Tony Chachere's Cajun seasoning into your bag. A little dash at the table is an easy way to make any dish taste just the way you like it.
